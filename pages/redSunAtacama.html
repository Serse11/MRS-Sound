Born in 2014 in Paris, France, RED SUN ATACAMA combine desert rock psychedelia and punk fury. Volcanic and wild, Chilean Atacama Desert mesmerizes the band as much as it inspires them. Their sound is driven by its beyond compare magnetism. They self-produce their first EP ‘Part 1’ in 2015, and release a bubbling debut album untitled ‘Licancabur’ in 2018 via More Fuzz Records. The record definitely establishes the trio’s unique, raw and insolent style.

On stage, Red Sun Atacama fully manage to share the same hypnotic outburst than on their studio efforts: they already performed in various European countries alongside international heavyweights such as Uncle Acid And The Dead Beats, Mars Red Sky, Planet of Zeus, Steak, Slift…

In 2022, Red Sun Atacama sign to Mrs Red Sound (Mars Red Sky’s imprint) for the release of their sophomore studio album. The explosive new album ‘DARWIN’ is driven by an overflowing desert rock, stoner and punk energy, and inspired by the Andean magic. It was recorded and mixed by Amaury Sauvé (Birds In Row) at The Apiary studio (Laval, France). Sauvé also gave them a hand with additional percussions. The record is coming out on June 17th on Mrs Red Sound Records. Vinyl release on September 16th.
